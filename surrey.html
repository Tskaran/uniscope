📝 IELTS Waiver Criteria
  • 📘
    CBSE / ISC Boards
    Minimum 70% in English subject
    ✓ IELTS Waiver Granted
  • 📙
    Indian State Boards
    Minimum 80% in English
    ✓ Eligible for IELTS 6.5 (no band below 6.0)
    Minimum 85% in English
    ✓ Eligible for IELTS 7.0 (no band below 6.5)

New Update — Effective February 2026 Intake
IELTS waivers are now available for Andhra Pradesh and Telangana state boards, provided the percentage criteria above are met.
* Note: English language scores must have been achieved within 5 years of the course start date.
